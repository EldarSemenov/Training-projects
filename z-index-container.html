Z-index. Description

My task was to make the child-2 on the top of all blocks. If we just add z-index to this child, it's not gonna work since its parent on the bottom of the stacking context. To achive the result I just added z-index to his parent on hover pseudo class to make its child-2 on the top of all elements. And also I gave z-index: 2; to the child-2 to be higher than its parent. Place your cursour on the navigation purple block. See also isolation method.

P.S. The light-blue hidden circle that you see has the position absolute, his parent footer container has overflow: hidden. If you work with the < body >overflow:hidden;, add the same to the < html >. Also its working if you use overflow: auto;
